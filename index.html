ABOUT US

DotWord is a private company recently established in the year 2017 by three rural youngsters and soon to operate. Former IT student; Mzoxolo Londa, former public relations student; Lukholo Luzipho and a restaurant manager; Luthando Nyameni who is also a director and a co-founder of a construction company.

DotWorld, allocated in Umtata No. 61 Park home, is the perfect base for creative, innovative, vibrant, young network and have a unique cup of coffee at the same time.

We will offer the community easy and affordable access to internet. DotWord will fully provide access to various intranet applications such as, WWW, FTP, email etc. Will also provide unique printing of T-shirts and cultural cloth designs, moreover provide coffee environment with its freshly baked bakery items to our customers.
